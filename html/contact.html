Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services

How long does it typically take to complete a website?
The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple website might take 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects can take 1-3 months. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.
What information do you need to get started on a project?
To get started, I'll need details about your business, your goals for the website, your target audience, any design preferences, and content (text, images, etc.) that you want to include. Don't worry if you don't have everything ready—I can guide you through the process.
Do you provide website maintenance services?
Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. These can include regular updates, backups, security monitoring, and content updates as needed.
What is your payment structure?
I typically require a 50% deposit to secure your project in my schedule, with the remaining balance due upon completion. For larger projects, we can establish a milestone-based payment schedule. All payment terms will be clearly outlined in our agreement.
Can you help with web hosting and domain registration?
Absolutely! I can recommend reliable hosting providers, help with domain registration, and handle all the technical aspects of setting up your website. I'll ensure everything is properly configured for optimal performance and security.
